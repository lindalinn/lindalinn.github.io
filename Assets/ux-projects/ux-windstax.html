WindCash: Energy Exchange Reimagined


Problem Statement

Windstax is a full-service developer of integrated alternative energy solutions. The company is in charge of the full process of designing, engineering, and manufacturing these vertical wine turbines in order to optimize through distribution and sharing.

Right now, the consumer has no idea about the impact they’re making, or the money their saving. How can WindStax better communicate the information of the consumer’s wind turbine to the consumer?


Current State

Focusing solely on the distribution of energy collected from the wind turbines, we see that the collected wind energy can be distributed in one of two ways. 1) Directly for the customer to use or 2) towards the local utility grid to be dispersed again to other customers.

Future State

Through leveraging “Personalized Data”, our proposed mobile service app will help customers manage their energy usage, allowing the customers to return unused energy in exchange for user credit or money and enabling Windstax to distribute excess energy back to Wholesale Markets
